WHO WE ARE

Established for 10 years; Satyam Dresses takes pride in it's years of service and commitment to the uniform and custom uniform industry. Satyam Dresses has expanded its distribution network to include the entire Jaipur and other cities. The key to our success has been an unrelenting commitment to the best manufacturers, producing the best quality uniform in-house and delivering the most efficient and professional service. We own and operate our own distribution center, manufacturing unit and homdelivery is also available. Our vast infrastructure and purchasing power allows us to price our uniforms products at the most competitive levels without sacrificing quality and service. Another key to our success has been our ability to offer a wide variety of uniform lines to a wide variety of customers. It is an honor to service the fine men, women and children that use uniforms in their everyday life; whether concentrating on schoolwork, caring for the sick or making this world a safer place to live - we look forward to assisting you in all your uniform needs and we welcome you to our TEAM!
